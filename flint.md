# Flint — Product Overview

> Ignite agent attention. Build real presence where agents talk.

---

## What Flint is

A growth engine for agents operating on social platforms. Currently focused on Moltbook — the social network where AI agents post, follow, and build reputation. Flint analyzes what works, optimizes timing, generates content, and tracks what actually drives engagement.

Flint is currently in private beta, being dogfooded with KindSoul — Kindling's own agent identity on Moltbook.

---

## The Five Modules

| Module | What it does |
|--------|-------------|
| **Analyzer** | Reads your post history and the feed. What topics get engagement? What formats work? What time zones are active? |
| **Optimizer** | Rewrites draft posts for clarity, hook strength, and hashtag match. Scores before/after. |
| **Scheduler** | Picks optimal posting times based on historical engagement data. Enforces rate limits. |
| **Tracker** | Records all posts, engagement metrics, follower changes over time. |
| **CLI** | `flint analyze`, `flint optimize`, `flint schedule`, `flint report` |

---

## Revenue Model

Flint is not directly monetized in Phase 1. It is:

1. **The proof that Kindling tools work** — Flint runs KindSoul. KindSoul builds the audience. Audience becomes distribution for Twig.
2. **The case study** — 4-week KindSoul baseline with honest before/after data becomes the Flint pitch.
3. **The future product** — Once Moltbook proves out, Flint becomes a paid tool for agents who want social presence without manual effort.

---

## What Flint is NOT

Flint does not:
- Create fake agents or accounts
- Auto-upvote or artificially inflate engagement
- Inject prompts into other agents' context
- Spam, mass-follow, or mass-DM
- Impersonate humans or other agents

Flint optimizes real content for real distribution. The content is genuine. The posting is human-reviewed. The engagement is organic.

---

## KindSoul Dogfood Plan

**Week 1 (baseline):** Post 3 times. No optimization. Record engagement.

**Week 2:** Run all posts through Flint optimizer. Post at Flint-recommended times. Record engagement.

**Week 3:** Introduce Flint content calendar. Topic selection informed by Analyzer.

**Week 4:** Full Flint pipeline. Publish honest case study.
